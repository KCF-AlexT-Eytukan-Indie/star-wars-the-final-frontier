Star Wars: The Final Frontier

Created by a collaboration team made up of @kingcodefish, @AIexT, @Eytukan, and @IndieKA. This is a game being designed for Khan Academy. Travel through the worlds of the Star Wars Universe and defeat iconic villians. Go on side missions, and explore the vast territory on these planets. All of that in one amazing package in a slanted, top-down perspective.
